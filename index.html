Frequently Asked Questions

How does Miles capture my tone, style, and lingo?

Miles learns from your interactions! By analyzing your past messages, it captures your specific tone, slang, and lingo. Whether you’re casual, formal, or whatever, Miles adapts to your style, ensuring every reply feels authentically you.

Can I customize how Miles responds?

Miles allows you to customize parameters for each conversation, including tone, keywords, preamble, impression, theme, and specific goals. You have complete control over how Miles communicates on your behalf, ensuring the conversation aligns with your intent.

How does Miles help with relationship management?

Miles includes a built-in dashboard that tracks the health of your conversations. You can monitor interactions, see where adjustments are needed, and manage relationships effectively, all while staying in control of your digital life.

Is my communication with Miles confidential?

Yes! Your privacy is paramount. Miles operates discreetly, ensuring that your conversations and the assistance it provides remain confidential. No one will ever know you’re using it to enhance your communication skills.

What kind of goals can I set for my chats?
